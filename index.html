About me

Hi there! I’m a bike messenger by day, an aspiring researcher by night, and this is my website. I live in Champaign, IL, have a great dog named Cooper, and love traveling. I obtained my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Currently, I am working on my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Martin Ostoja-Starzewski and Prof. Jianming Jin. My research is focused on the development of accurate and efficient algorithms for analyzing composites. It serves as a cornerstone for investigating effective properties—and it is computationally-oriented, bringing together the disciplines of computer science, computational nanomaterials science, and electromagnetics.
