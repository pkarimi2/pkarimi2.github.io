About me

Hello! I'm Pouyan Karimi — an engineer with a Ph.D. in Engineering Mechanics from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, with a minor in Electrical and Computer Engineering. My doctoral research, conducted under the supervision of Prof. Martin Ostoja-Starzewski , focused on computational modeling of advanced materials and random media using high-performance computing and finite element methods. I developed scalable simulation tools to analyze structural and physical behavior across multiple scales, including collaborative work with Boeing. I hold a Master’s degree in Structural Engineering (Engineering Mechanics) from the University of Tehran. My background combines deep theoretical insight with practical experience in structural analysis, materials modeling, and scientific computing. I’m passionate about structural design, numerical simulation, and building software tools that solve real engineering challenges.
