Alignment and Polydispersity of Nanocomposites

The goal of this project is to develop reliable computational algorithms to accurately predict the electrical properties of polydisperse nanocomposites. Disordered fields of fillers will be generated using the random sequential addition (RSA) algorithm, with the desired fillers added one by one such that the most recently added particle does not collide with the previously added ones; the process is continued until the desired volume fraction is reached. The combination of fillers and filler alignment effect on the electrical properties of nanocomposites was modeled. I gratefully acknowledge the use of the Taub cluster resources provided under the Computational Science and Engineering (CSE) program at the University of Illinois.
